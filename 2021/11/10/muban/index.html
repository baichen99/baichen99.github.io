话题引起

  1. The bar chart above present diferrent proportions/factors of consumers’ concern on choosing restaurant in a certain city in xx year.
  2. The line chart shows the ascending/ tendency of 主题

图表描述

  1. To put it more precise，描述变化或占比
  2. 静态图描述占比：the proportion of xxx in 2013 was 33% compared to 25% in 2008, showing an increase of 8%.
  3. “分别占用” the percentage of A B C and others is a, b, c, d respectively.
  4. 描述变化：the number/percentage of museums in China increases/decreases steadily/sharply from xx in 2013 to xx in 2015.
  5. 描述最值：xxx is the biggest proportion of all categories/factors

​ Exercising by themselves occupies the dominant position, accounting for 54.3%

  1. 多个描述时用while/whereas，in the meanwhile
  2. the proportion of those .., while that of those…

图表总结

  1. As a matter of fact, what author tries to tell us is more than statistics.
  2. Isolated as the figures seem to be, as a matter of fact, they are connected to one another closely.

具体意义

  1. In fact, this chart is a miniature of a prevalent phenomenon. An incresing number of customers choose …(描述)
  2. In fact, this chart is a miniature of contemporary society.
  3. The table leads us to find out what causes the …

意义论证

  1. I can find no better reasons than the following ones./There are many reasons for this phenomenon.（解释原因）
    1. On one hand./To begin with/For one thing
    2. On the other hand./Furthermore/For another
  • 原因，which leads to/result in the downward/upward tendency of…

意义总结

  1. （正面/中立）As a consequence/result
  2. （负面）If we keep turing a blind eye to it, the problem above will generate severe consequences. For example,..

结尾段

引言+结论

  1. Considering every aspect of this phenomenon, I, as most of others will do, safely draw a conclusion that…,it requires a xxx

总结+建议+憧憬

  1. 号召：Considering every aspect of this phenonmenon, we should bear in mind that … , we should xxx, Only in this way can we meet a brighter furture.
  2. 两面：(If used carelessly or without restraint), xx could prove to be a double-edged sword. Therefore, we must absorb the essence and neglect the drawbacks of xxx Only in this way can we ….

​ / take full advantage of merit of xx, such as…, and avoid the drawbacks such as …

  1. 中立： No matter what the reason/Whether it is positive or negative, one thing is certian that there is a growing tendency xxx, which I believe will not change in a short time. + 憧憬

    In can be predicted that as … improves/develops, xx can be more 发展趋势

憧憬

  1. With suporting policies and rational(理性的) instructions, it is reasonable for us to believe that our society can achieve a more healthy development.
